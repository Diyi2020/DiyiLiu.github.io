Welcome! I'm a Postdoctoral Researcher at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, working with Bert de Jong, Chao Yang, and Lin Lin.

My research is in **computational and applied mathematics**, with interests in **numerical analysis**, **mathematical physics**, and **quantum information**.

I work on mathematical and computational problems motivated by **quantum simulation**, **quantum error correction**, and **condensed matter physics**.

## Selected Research Directions

### Quantum Simulation and Block Encoding
I study efficient algorithms for simulating many-body quantum systems, with a recent focus on block encoding and resource-efficient Hamiltonian simulation.

Selected papers:
- [Block Encoding for Pairing Hamiltonian]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2024-06-15-block-encoding-pairing)
- [Block Encoding with Low gate count for second quantized Hamiltonian]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2025-02-01-block-encoding-low-gate)

### Magnus Expansion and Time-Dependent Hamiltonians
I am interested in the analysis of time-dependent quantum dynamics, especially high-order methods and superconvergence results for Schrödinger-type problems.

Selected papers:
- [Time-dependent Hamiltonian Simulation via Magnus Expansion and Superconvergence for the Schrödinger equation]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2024-12-15-time-dependent-hamiltonian)
- [High order Magnus Expansion for Hamiltonian Simulation]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2025-01-15-high-order-magnus)

### Moire Materials and Data-Driven Modeling
Another part of my work connects numerical analysis and machine learning with electronic-structure questions in twisted and moire materials.

Selected papers:
- [Learning the local density of states of a bilayer moire material]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2023-09-01-learning-local-density)
- [Quantum dynamics of Twisted bilayer graphene]({{ site.baseurl }}/publication/2024-03-15-quantum-dynamics-twisted)

## Academic Background

I received my **Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics** from the University of Minnesota and my **B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics** from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
