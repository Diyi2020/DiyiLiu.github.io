This paper develops efficient quantum circuits for block encoding second-quantized Hamiltonians while minimizing the gate count, which is crucial for implementing quantum algorithms on near-term quantum devices. The work combines techniques from quantum simulation and circuit synthesis to achieve improved resource efficiency.
